# Improvement Ideas

## Inflexibility on Skill Level in Penultimate Weeks

Basing study session on Skill level becomes inflexible in penultimate weeks when covering multiple skills within one quiz.

Potential solution: Track time based on domain instead of skill level.  The test results show performance by exam section (domain) and not by skill level.

## Structure of Study Note Artifacts

Create NotebookLM prompt to produce the study note in a more structured, applicable format.

## Study Action "Complete"

Doesn't log time entries. Revisit on whether 'Complete' is needed.

## Exam Prep

Consider total number of days to prepare for exam: 60

Understand total number of tasks: e.g. 85

Understand hours per day commitment: e.g. 2 hours per day

Understand hours per task:

- Intermediat exam: 1.5 hours per task
- Advanced exam: 2.5 hours per task

Task can be divided into two parts:

- NotebookLM quizzes
- Topic review guide
