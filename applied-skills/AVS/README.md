## Explore

Use the study log as the source of truth for each session. Follow questions wherever they lead, and capture useful findings, decisions, blockers, and next steps in Notes.

Possible areas to explore include:

- AVS architecture: private clouds, clusters, hosts, and the ESXi/vCenter/NSX-T/vSAN stack.
- Private cloud deployment, capacity planning, and Generation 1 vs Generation 2 differences.
- Connectivity: ExpressRoute, Global Reach, hub-and-spoke, Virtual WAN, and jump-box access.
- NSX-T networking: segments, gateways, distributed firewall, and route planning.
- Workload migration with HCX, including replication, network extension, and cutover.
- Monitoring, alerting, and operations for AVS private clouds.
